The story

Two twins. One flame.

Nobody handed us a studio. We’re 22-year-old twins who wanted in on boutique fitness, so we built our own and opened the door for everyone else too.

The twins

Founded at 22, mid-semester.

Twin Flames is exactly what it sounds like: a hot pilates studio founded by twins. We’re 22-year-old college students who loved the boutique pilates experience and hated the pressure of expensive, long-term memberships that usually came with it.

So we built a studio where that pressure doesn’t exist: welcoming, inclusive, and unapologetically ours. An upbeat, edgy, club-like atmosphere where you can move, sweat, and connect.

Bring your “twin flame” and let’s get hot.
