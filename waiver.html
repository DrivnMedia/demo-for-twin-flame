Before your first class

Two things before you hit the mat.

One’s a form, one’s a habit. Sign the waiver once, know how to handle the heat, and your first class takes care of itself.

Thing one

Sign the waiver.

Takes about 2 minutes, one time only. It’s required before your first class, so knock it out now and walk straight in.

Sign the Waiver

Thing two

Handle the heat.

The room runs 95–100° and the heat is part of the workout. Here’s the studio’s guide to doing it right.

Before class

  • Hydrated today (water + electrolytes recommended)
  • Feeling well and free of illness
  • Water bottle with you

During class

  • Listen to your body
  • Take breaks whenever needed
  • Sip water as needed
  • Leave the room at any time if you feel unwell

Stop immediately & notify your instructor if you experience:

  • Dizziness or lightheadedness
  • Nausea
  • Chest pain
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Confusion or disorientation
  • Severe muscle cramping
  • Feeling faint

Honor your body.

There is no award for pushing through symptoms. Modify, rest, cool down, or leave the room whenever needed. Your safety comes first. Questions or concerns? Please speak with your instructor before class.

That’s it
