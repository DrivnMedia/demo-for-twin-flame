First time?

Come ready.

The only rule that matters: bring your own mat and towel. The rest is just us making your first class easier.

  • Sign the waiver before you arrive2 minutes, one time. Sign it here, along with our heat safety guide.
  • Bring your mat + towelStudio policy: every class is BYO mat and towel. You’ll want both.
  • Bring waterThe room sits at 95–100°. Hydrate before, during, and after.
  • Arrive a few minutes earlyGet your spot, meet your instructor, let your body warm into the heat.
  • No experience neededAll levels, every class. Your instructor will meet you where you are.

Heads up: class playlists are loud and may contain explicit lyrics.
